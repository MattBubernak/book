What is the name of your app?
WeBuildIt

What collaborative activity can people do together using your app?
Users can live update(or even video stream) building or fixing tasks and get help from the online community. Users can register as experts and provide advise, or simply watch!


What is the ideal number of concurrent users (must be at least 10 but no more than 40)?
Around 1-10 max for helping, but any number for viewing/commenting.

What skills do people need in order to do this activity?
WeBuildIt can be used for building, or fixing anything. Ideally, we want experts in specific tasks(fixing a bike, fixing a car, etc)

Is this activity for fun or for something practical?
This activity is for practical use.

How can a person see what everyone else is doing?
Live updates and possibly a live stream.

How can a person see the most recent result of everyone's collaborative effort?
Users provide questions/or status updates. The newest ones show up first.

What can an admin see (i.e., God's mode)?
Admins can see a list of all of the current projects, with links to them. They also have acces to approving/denying the registered experts.
